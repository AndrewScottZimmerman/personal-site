# Decisions
## Why Figma for design?
- Online saves, easy to use, industry standard in many cases.
- Free
- Platform agnostic.
- Google font integration.

## Why Nuxt?
- Vue 3 but with a more developed framework for SEO, routing, etc.
- Makes life easier.

## Why CloudFlare?
- Security protocols are easy.
- Domain is easy to get.
- Billing is straightforward.
- Affordable.

## Why AWS?
- Affordable.
- Easy to scale.
- Demonstration of a common pipline for data handling.
- Common platform.
